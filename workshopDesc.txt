[h1]If you're a user[/h1]
This doesnt do anything on it's own, the modders are supposed to use this to adjust players carry weight without conflicting with each other.


[h1]If you're a modder[/h1]
You are probably aware that majority of mods that modify carry weight conflict with each other and usually it's just "whichever mod applied their override last" sets the weight.

So I made this little thing.

- This allows multiple mods to modify carry weight without overwriting each other changes.
- Supports flat bonuses and multiplications for both base weight (before str applied) and total max weight (after str bonuses are applied)

So something like this is possible and very easy to do now: 

- Mod A has sandbox setting that sets base carry weight to 10 (from 8)
- Mod B has a trait that gives player 0.25 bonus to base carry weight per point of strength
- Mod C has a exoskeleton that when worn increases total carry weight by 50%.

There's a file that gives examples on how to use this framework,

Contents\mods\Unified Carry Weight Framework\42.15\media\lua\client\UCWF_example.lua.txt

Just remove .txt so your IDE makes it easier to read.
